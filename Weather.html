WEATHER

Humans can influence the weather through various activities that contribute to climate change, altering long-term patterns and conditions. Key ways in which human activities impact the weather include: greenhouse gasses, deforestation and also air pollution

Neglecting the consequences of climate change, driven by human activities such as excessive greenhouse gas emissions, deforestation, and unsustainable practices, poses severe risks to humans, leading to more frequent and intense extreme weather events, rising sea levels, disruptions in food and water resources, and increased health hazards.

Humans can positively impact the weather by adopting sustainable practices, reducing greenhouse gas emissions, promoting afforestation, and embracing environmentally conscious behaviors to mitigate climate change and foster a more stable and resilient global climate.
